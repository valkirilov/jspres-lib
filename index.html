What is jspres-lib?
It's a presentation framework for creating a simple presentations which lives in the modern browser
What can do?
As you can see this tool lets you decorate your talk with simple and pretty background
Only that?
And as you saw it provides you with a simple set of transitions and animations which you can use
Which transitions?
You can slide in and out from the all edges of screen, also zoom in and out or just play with fading
Good news
You can use our stuff and this will boost you to make your presentation faster
OR
you can make your own animations...
And this is easy!
Because everything is based on the power of the CSS!
You've got a few text templates too
  • You can use our predefined styles to make your presentation
  • looks good without wasting your time in making positioning and etc.
  • But you can use everything, we don't stop you!
